Plantain and Egg sauce

Fried Plantain and Egg sauce recipe is a palatable meal of all time and season.It is a simple easy dish to cook. Plantain and egg sauce is a common breakfast food in most homes.

Nigeria egg sauce with fried plantain is my favourite meal;moreover,they are naturally sweet food; and a good source of energy needed in the body

Plantain is classified as starchy food and ripe plantain can as well be eaten raw.In other word, plantain recipe with egg is a healthy combination of carbohydrate and protein.Plantain is rich in vitamin B6; the unripe plantains also refer to as green plantain is high in Vitamin C,A and K,Magnessium and Potassium

In Nigeria,plantains are seasonal fruits and could be very expensive when is out of season. You can make different delicacies with plantain and egg and they are;plantain omellete, Plantain and egg sandwich, plantain egg frittata, and fried plantain pizza.

Ingredients/Measurements

S/N Ingredients Measurement
1 Ripe plantain 2
2 Fresh Tomatoes 4
3 Scotch bonnet peppers 4
3 Eggs 3
4 Onion 1
5 Seasoning Cube 1
6 Vegetable oil for frying 50ml
7 Salt to taste 1 tablespoon

Methods of preparing Fried-plantain and egg sauce

  1. First of all, wash your plantains to remove any dirt or sand from the skin. Thereafter, peel the back and slice into choice shape and size
  2. Wash the tomatoes, onions and pepper, then finely slice your tomatoes, pepper, and onions in a plate, keep aside for later use
  3. Heat your vegetable oil in a frying pan, sprinkle salt into the sliced plantain, toss repeatedly to mix. Stir in the plantain into the hot oil and fry until color turns golden brown, remove from heat into a sieve allow the oil to drain
  4. Reduce the quantity of oil in the frying pan, heat again and stir in the slice onions; stir fry for like 30 seconds before sliced adding the tomatoes and pepper. Fry for about 3-5 minutes, add the seasoning cube and salt to taste, whish the eggs and pour into mixture.
  5. Stir to mix properly with the tomatoe stew for 2 minutes, and then remove from the heat, and then you can serve your delicious yummy fried plantain with egg sauce
